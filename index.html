STORIES BEHIND THE WHEEL

BMW 3/15

MARCH

1929

First car introduced to the world

The first BMW car, the BMW 3/15, was launched in March 1929, based on the British Austin Seven. Produced at BMW’s Eisenach plant, it was a compact, affordable car that marked the company’s entry into automobile manufacturing. This milestone set the stage for BMW’s growth into a global leader in luxury and performance vehicles.

BMW 326

February

1936

Luxury and Comfort: Experiences

The BMW 326, introduced on February 15, 1936, was BMW's first car focused on passenger comfort, featuring advanced suspension, hydraulic brakes, and a spacious interior. .

BMW 328

June

1936

Cars that Adapt to Your Lifestyle

The BMW 328, introduced in June 1936, was BMW’s first true sports car, celebrated for its lightweight design, powerful engine, and agile handling. It quickly gained fame for its performance in racing, helping establish BMW’s reputation in the world of motorsports. The 328’s innovative engineering set the foundation for BMW’s future sports cars and remains a classic icon today
